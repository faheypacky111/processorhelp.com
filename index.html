About

Built from scar tissue.

Processor Help is a payment processing solutions company. We help businesses navigate payment processor approvals — from first-time solopreneurs to enterprise operators managing complex processor portfolios.

Processor Help was founded by an operator who spent seven years in the e-commerce trenches. What started as a response to watching good businesses get gutted overnight by payment processor shutdowns became something bigger — a realization that the entire payment processor decision process is broken at every stage of a business.

The solopreneur launching their first product has no idea which processors will accept their vertical. They apply blindly to Stripe, get approved, and three months later discover their industry triggers holds and freezes. The damage is already done.

The established brand owner scaling from $30K/month to $300K/month stays with their original processor out of inertia — paying rates built for a business they no longer run, missing volume-tier pricing, ignoring better acquirers that would serve them properly.

The enterprise operator manages a patchwork of MIDs across regions and verticals but has never systematically mapped which processors would actually perform best for each slice of their business. Decisions get made by whoever has a warm contact, not by data.

And then there's the brand that just got shut down — the scramble, the rejections, the weeks of bleeding revenue while applications sit in underwriting limbo.

We built Processor Help because every one of these scenarios has the same root cause: the information exists but it's fragmented across fifty websites, trade publications, forum threads, and the memories of agents who don't return your calls.

Every payment processor has published risk tolerances, volume minimums, vertical restrictions, and approval criteria. We concentrated that knowledge into an engine. That runs in ninety seconds. For ten dollars. Whether you're filing your first LLC or managing enterprise payment infrastructure.

We're not a payment processor. We're not a broker. We don't take commissions. We don't submit your application for you. We're a compass — an independent advisory tool that tells you which doors are open, which are closed, and why. You walk through the doors yourself.

Processor Help is independently owned and built in Milwaukee. No investors, no agendas, no affiliations with any processor on our platform. The only customer we answer to is the person who paid ten dollars expecting the truth.
