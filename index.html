Payment Information: Email address, cardholder name, and payment card details submitted during checkout. Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment provider. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

Technical Information: Basic usage data including IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, collected through standard web analytics.

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to customer support inquiries

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your business profile with payment processors. When you click "Apply" on any processor in your report, you are redirected to that processor's own website, where you apply directly. Processor Help does not submit applications on your behalf.

We may share information with our service providers as described in Section 4a below, and with legal authorities if required by law.

We use carefully selected third-party services to operate Processor Help. These providers receive only the data necessary to perform their function, are bound by data protection agreements, and may not use your information for their own purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not share your data with payment processors listed in our underwriting engine — when you click "Apply at [Processor]," you are redirected to their website to apply directly.

In the event of a confirmed data breach affecting your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users via email within 72 hours of confirming the breach (in line with GDPR requirements)
  • Provide details about the nature of the breach, the data potentially affected, and the steps we are taking
  • Offer guidance on protective steps you can take
  • Notify applicable regulatory authorities as required by federal and state law (including all 50 US state breach notification laws)
  • Cooperate fully with investigations and provide identity protection resources where appropriate

Free accounts (users who have signed up but not subscribed) are subject to the same privacy protections as paid accounts. We collect only the information you provide (business name, email, password) and store it securely. Free accounts that remain inactive for 12 consecutive months may be archived; we will email you 30 days before any data removal.

5. Data Retention

We retain your application data for 12 months to allow you to re-access your report and to improve our underwriting engine. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. Payment records are retained per applicable financial and tax regulations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@processorhelp.com.

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for all data transmission and secure infrastructure for storage. No system is perfectly secure — you provide information at your own risk.
